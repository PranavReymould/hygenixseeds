Welcome to Hygenix, where innovation meets agricultural excellence. In a world where the demand for vegetables rich in antioxidants and essential plant chemicals, continues to rise, Hygenix emerges as a pioneer in providing high-yield, disease-tolerant vegetable seeds to meet the growing needs of farmers across the nation. Founded through the collaboration of Shri YVR and Shri M S Reddy, who is a vegetable seed man, Hygenix specialises on vegetable hybrids that meet the requirements of farmers in different agro-climatic zones of the country.
